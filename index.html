Controls

🖥️ Keyboard Controls:

← → Arrow Keys: Move Left and Right

↑ or Space: Jump

X: Attack

📱 Mobile Controls: Use on-screen buttons at the bottom

Game Objectives

🪙 Collect coins to increase your score

🗝️ Find keys to progress to the next level

💥 Defeat enemies by jumping on them or attacking

⚡ Collect power-ups for special abilities
